Rings

Tom Brady won Super Bowl XXXVI, XXXVIII, XLIX, and LI. He is the only quarterback to ever win five Super Bowls.

Tom Brady is also the only quarterback to win four Super Bowl MVP Awards.

Tom Brady holds the Super Bowl records for most touchdown passes, most passing yards, most passes attempted and completed, most passes completed in a Super Bowl and most passing yards in a single Super Bowl

As you can clearly see, no one is better than Brady in the clutch.
