Interests

Outdoor

I enjoy many outdoor activities. I've been skiing ever since I was old enough to strap into a harness. My home mountain is Sugarloaf, although I think Saddleback has the best snow on the east coast.

I also grew up hiking frequently, with the Appalachian trail less than an hour drive away I've been on many hiking and backpacking trips.

Growing up in Maine instilled a lifelong passion for fishing in me. While I prefer lobstering to fishing with a rod and a reel, nothing makes me happier than being out on a boat catching some fish.

I also enjoy golfing. I grew up playing at Wilson Lake Country Club in Wilton, Maine. I was the captain my senior year, as well as my freshman year at Wells College.
